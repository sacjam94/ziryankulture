The Secret of Idi Irachi

Our Idi Irachi is made from pounded, dried, and spiced premium buffalo meat. A perfect blend of traditional spices ensures every bite is packed with fiery, authentic flavor.

The Artisanal Narrative

Instead of tying products to specific communities, the brand highlights the geographic and historical techniques of the Kottayam and Pala belts. It celebrates the collective wisdom of generations who perfected the art of sun-drying, pan-drying, and spice-blending, treating these methods as a shared regional treasure.

Traditional Preservation

Specializing in heritage products like Ediyirachi (pan-dried buffalo meat), pickles, and sun-dried items.

Aesthetic Philosophy

Adopting a "Quiet Luxury" and "Modern Vintage" design language. This involves high-contrast typography and minimalist packaging that contrasts with the rustic, homemade nature of the products.

Cultural Preservation

Focusing on cultural heritage through a clean, minimalist lens, moving beyond specific iconography to celebrate the broader artisanal history of the region.

The Ziryan Kulture approaches heritage not as a static artifact, but as a living, breathing craft. By moving beyond specific social identifiers, the brand centers its identity on the universal artistry of the Kerala region.
